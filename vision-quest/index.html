The Wilderness Quest

The Wilderness Quest offers elements of the traditional vision quest rite-of-passage ceremony. The full quest includes four days and four nights alone in the wilderness, supported by preparation, reflection, and integration.

The practice supports clarity, inner listening, personal transformation, and a deeper connection with spirit and nature.

  • Preparation
  • Solitude
  • Reflection
  • Integration
