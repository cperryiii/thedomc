On the Practice

What a Vision Quest is

A Vision Quest offers an intentional nature-based spiritual practice involving preparation, time in solitude, deep self-reflection, and integration. The process supports clarity, inner listening, personal transformation, and deeper connection with spirit and nature.

The Day Quest and full Vision Quest that follow offer a deeper opportunity to step into this work through direct experience.

Your Guide

Michele Angelini

Michele works as a wilderness quest guide, homeopathic practitioner, and PhD candidate in East-West Psychology at the California Institute of Integral Studies. She also brings Vision Quest experience through Native elders.

Her work brings together Indigenous ways of healing, wilderness quest practices, nature-based healing, and homeopathy.
